Verified scheme discovery for farmers (State + District)

How this helps for scheme applications

  • Find verified schemes using your profile and district.
  • Prepare key details: name, Aadhaar, mobile, bank account, IFSC, and land details.
  • Auto-extract details from uploaded Aadhaar and passbook where possible.
  • Open the official portal. This assistant tells you what information to fill and where to fill it, then you submit manually.

How to access official scheme portals

  • Open the official portal from each recommended scheme.
  • Keep Aadhaar, mobile number, bank account, IFSC, and land details ready.
  • Use the scheme-specific ID asked on the portal (registration number, mobile, Aadhaar, etc.).
  • Complete OTP/eKYC or portal verification steps if prompted.
  • Use extracted details from this assistant to fill fields correctly before final submit.

Note: Login and verification steps vary by scheme and portal.
